Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. Introduction

makuro.io ("we," "our," or "us") provides an omnichannel messaging platform that allows users to connect multiple social media and communication platforms and respond to messages from a unified interface.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information, including message data, when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

  • Name, email address, and login credentials
  • Account preferences and settings

b. Connected Platform Data

When you connect third-party platforms (such as Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, or others), we may access:

  • Public profile information (name, username, profile picture)
  • Page or account metadata
  • Messages and conversations (including message content, sender information, timestamps, and attachments where permitted)

We only access this data based on the permissions you explicitly grant.

3. How We Use Message Data

Message and conversation data is used strictly for the purpose of providing core functionality, including:

  • Displaying messages from connected platforms in a unified inbox
  • Enabling you to read and reply to messages
  • Synchronizing message status (e.g., read/unread)
  • Facilitating real-time communication across platforms

We do not:

  • Use message content for advertising
  • Sell or share message data with third parties for marketing
  • Analyze message content beyond what is necessary to deliver the service

4. Data Storage and Processing

  • Messages may be temporarily processed and stored to enable delivery, synchronization, and performance optimization
  • We minimize storage duration and retain message data only as necessary to provide the service
  • Where possible, messages are fetched and displayed in real time without long-term storage

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data.

We may share limited data with:

  • Third-party platform APIs (e.g., Meta) to send and receive messages on your behalf
  • Infrastructure providers (hosting, databases) strictly for service operation
  • Legal authorities if required by law

6. Data Retention

  • Message data is retained only as long as necessary to provide messaging functionality
  • Users may disconnect accounts or request deletion of their data at any time
  • Upon disconnection, associated message data will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable timeframe

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect message data and account information, including encryption in transit and access controls.

However, no system is completely secure.

8. User Control and Rights

You have control over your data:

  • You can connect or disconnect platforms at any time
  • You can request deletion of your data
  • You can revoke permissions directly from the connected platform

Contact: info@bdpl.com.np

9. Third-Party Platforms

makuro.io integrates with third-party messaging platforms. Your use of those services is also subject to their respective privacy policies.

We only use data received from these platforms in accordance with their platform policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with an updated revision date.

13. Meta (Facebook) Platform Data Usage

makuro.io's use of data received from Meta APIs will comply with all applicable Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies.

Specifically:

  • Data accessed via Meta APIs (including messages) is used only to provide user-requested messaging functionality
  • We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or resale
  • We do not transfer Meta data to third parties except as necessary to operate the service
  • Users can request deletion of their data at any time

14. Data Deletion Instructions

Users may request deletion of their data by:

  • Emailing: info@bdpl.com.np
  • Disconnecting their accounts via the platform settings

Upon request, we will delete associated data within a reasonable timeframe unless retention is required by law.
